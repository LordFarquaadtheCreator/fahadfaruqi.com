Prototype energy is valuable because it creates momentum. But the product usually emerges later, when the team starts asking quieter questions.

Where does the page shift? Which request is slow? Which form label makes the next action ambiguous? Which state is technically valid but emotionally confusing?

Those details are not polish as decoration. They are polish as reliability.
